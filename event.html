Education & Empower

We embrace the era of digital education through our innovative approach of providing digital classes.They actively practice cutting-edge IT technologies, enabling them to stay ahead in today's rapidly evolving digital landscape. Moreover, students enthusiastically participate in book review competitions, showcasing their critical thinking skills and fostering a love for literature.

Hygiene for kids

We furnish students with essential amenities such as soap and toothpaste. Moreover, the diligent laundering of attire is meticulously supervised by our group leaders, ensuring impeccable cleanliness and hygiene standards are maintained at all times.

Relief for Needy

We prioritize aiding those in need. Our efforts involve distributing essential relief items such as food, clothing, and other necessities during times of natural disasters, as well as during the celebratory occasions of Eid and various other significant events.we strive to provide meaningful support to individuals and communities during both challenging and joyous times.

Community Care Initiative

At CCI+, our Weekly Cleaning Initiative involves regular clean-up drives to maintain a hygienic environment. Our efforts extend beyond cleanliness as we raise community awareness. By proactively addressing potential breeding grounds, we've notably reduced dengue outbreaks, contributing to a safer and healthier community.

Electrician Traing Course

We are dedicated to nurturing students' skills holistically. Our Electrician Training Course is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical experience.students learn electrical systems and industry practices. Our goal is to empower graduates with the technical and practical expertise needed for success in the electrical engineering field.
